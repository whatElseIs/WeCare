"COHERENT BREATHING"

Coherent Breathing is based on a 5.5 second inhale followed by a 5.5 second exhale using your nose. There are many scientific benefits such as improving heart rate variability (HRV), reducing blood pressure, and inducing greater feelings of relaxation
  • Back

STEPS: Inhale for a count of 5. Exhale for a count of 5. Continue this breathing pattern for at least two minutes.
